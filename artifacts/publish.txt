Most comprehensive App available for Android. The app keeps a complete history of all your activity and helps to track your progress while participating in the club. This is an easy to use and fun to interact app, which makes it more exciting to organize and participate in the club.


This app helps you to play different roles like timer, grammarian, speaker, toast master during a ToastMasters meeting and  organize your speech notes, table topics and better plan for your meetings. 

Features Include
- Track all your club activity and club meetings
- Flexibile Timer
- amCounter
- Create flash cards for speech and table topics
- Manage club members
- Check your nervousness
and more.
